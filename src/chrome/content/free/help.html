The add-on allows to stick notes to emails. The notes don't affect the email in any way. They are stored locally on your hard drive (or on a centralized server in the Multi-User edition of the add-on).

Actions you may perform:
  • Add note to selected email message.
  • Edit note.
  • Delete note.
  • Set/Change note status
  • Search for notes containing given text.
  • Add the same note to multiple emails.
  • Tag messages which have notes.
All actions are accessible through hotkeys, which are customizable. Status icons are also customizable.

Additional details and information about updates is available on the product forum

If you share email account with somebody else or if you want to keep the notes synchronized among several computers then you may be interested in Multi-User edition of the add-on. The Multi-User edition allows to share and manage collaboratively your notes with other users of a shared email box.
